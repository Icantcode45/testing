How long does an IV therapy session take?

Each session typically lasts between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the type of treatment and individual needs. Our goal is to provide an efficient and comfortable experience.

Is IV therapy safe?

Yes, IV therapy is safe when administered by trained professionals. Our licensed staff use sterile materials and follow strict protocols to ensure each treatment is effective and secure.

  •  

How often should I get IV therapy?

Frequency depends on individual goals and health needs. Some clients choose weekly treatments for maintenance, while others schedule sessions as needed for hydration, recovery, or immune support.

Does IV therapy hurt?

Most clients feel minimal discomfort similar to a routine blood draw. Our professionals are skilled in making the process as quick and painless as possible.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, North Phoenix, Tempe, and Fountain Hills, bringing personalized IV therapy directly to your location for added convenience.
